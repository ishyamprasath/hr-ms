HR Tracker is a private internal hiring system built for companies that want to stop wasting money on Naukri and start using what they already have.

---

**Features**

Smart Search: HR types plain English queries. The AI understands intent not just keywords. Finds candidates who match what you mean even if they used different words in their CV.

Auto Match: The moment a new job requirement is created the system instantly surfaces the top 10 internal candidates. Zero manual work.

CV Chat: HR can open any candidate profile and ask questions in plain English. "What is his experience with IoT?" "Has she worked in a startup before?" Gemma reads the CV and answers immediately. No more opening PDF after PDF.

Profile Freshness Score: Every candidate gets a live score based on how recently their profile was updated. High score means shown first in search. Low score means pushed down automatically. The "10 year developer who still shows as a fresher in the system" problem is gone.

Auto Database Refresh: When a new job opens the system finds old matching candidates from 2 to 3 years ago and sends them a personalized WhatsApp message automatically. They reply with a new CV. System parses it and updates itself. HR is not involved at any point.

Auto Nudge Engine

At 30, 60, and 90 days, candidates who have not updated their profile get an automatic WhatsApp plus email reminder. Fully scheduled. Nobody has to remember to follow up.

Ethical Sourcing Extension: HR spots someone interesting on LinkedIn or GitHub. One click on the browser extension. A TVS branded consent invite is sent to that person. They click to agree. They enter the pool legally. No scraping. No legal risk.

Consent Certificate: Every candidate has a timestamped record of how and when they gave consent. If anyone ever questions where their data came from, HR pulls up the certificate instantly. Fully compliant with DPDP 2025.

Internal First Check: Before going outside, the system checks current employees too. Someone in one department might be perfect for a role in another. Surfaces automatically. Promotion and lateral transfer opportunities that would otherwise be missed.

Job Ad Image Generator: HR can generate job ad creatives directly inside the portal using FLUX.2-klein-4B running on HuggingFace Spaces. Type a prompt, get an image. Free via HF token.

Job Ad Video Generator: Short video ads for job postings generated using LTX-Video on HuggingFace Spaces. Free.

Cost Savings Dashboard: A real time view showing how many candidates were hired from the internal pool, how many Naukri postings were avoided, and exactly how much money was saved. Leadership can see the ROI in rupees at any time.

External Push: Last resort only. One click posts the JD to Naukri, LinkedIn, or Indeed. All incoming applicants are automatically pulled into the internal pool with consent captured. Paid for once, available forever.

**Tech Stack**

Frontend is Next.js 16 with Tailwind CSS deployed on Vercel for free.

Backend is FastAPI in Python hosted on Render free tier. Fast, async, built for AI integration.

Database is MongoDB Atlas free tier. 512MB free forever. Candidate profiles, job requirements, consent logs all fit naturally as JSON documents.

Vector Search is MongoDB Atlas Vector Search built directly into the database. No separate vector database needed. This is what powers the semantic Smart Search.

AI model is Gemma via Google AI Studio API. Completely free with no usage limits at this scale. Handles Smart Search, CV Chat, Auto Match, all conversational features.

Embeddings are generated by sentence-transformers running on the backend server. Open source, zero API cost. Converts every CV and job description into a vector for semantic comparison.

Resume parsing is done by pdfplumber and PyMuPDF. Free and open source. Extracts all text from any PDF CV automatically on upload.

Scheduling is handled by APScheduler in Python. Manages all the 30, 60, 90 day nudge jobs and auto-refresh triggers. No Redis needed.

WhatsApp automation uses wacli, a Go CLI tool built on the whatsmeow library. MIT licensed, completely free, 2.2k stars on GitHub, actively maintained with v0.6.0 released in April 2026. Runs on your personal WhatsApp number. Scan QR once and all automated messages fire from your number via subprocess calls from FastAPI.

Email is handled by Brevo with 300 free emails per day, no card required.

Image and video generation run on HuggingFace Spaces using HF token. Free.

Browser extension is built with Edge Manifest V3 and published on Microsoft Edge Addons. Free to publish and free forever.

Auth is NextAuth.js. Free.

Consent records are stored in MongoDB with timestamps and a PDF certificate generated on demand using reportlab. Free.

**What it does**

When HR raises a new requirement, the system immediately searches the internal candidate pool first. Not with keyword filters. With actual AI that understands what you mean. If you type "Python developer with EV experience" it will find someone whose CV says "Battery Management Systems" because it understands the intent behind your words.

If the right person is in the pool, great. HR gets the top 10 matches in seconds. No manual search. No cost.

If those matches are from 2 or 3 years ago and might be outdated, the system handles that too. It automatically sends them a WhatsApp message from your number. "Hi Arjun, you applied to us in 2023. We have a new role that fits your growth. Reply with your updated CV to be fast-tracked." They reply. The CV is parsed automatically. The database updates itself. HR does nothing.

If nobody in the pool fits, only then does it go to Naukri or LinkedIn. One click. The JD goes out. Every applicant who comes in is automatically pulled back into the internal pool with their consent recorded. You pay Naukri once. That person is yours forever.

---

**How the full workflow runs**

A candidate applies on the TVS Career Portal. Their CV is uploaded. pdfplumber extracts all the text. Skills, experience, location, years parsed out. Stored in MongoDB. sentence-transformers converts it to a vector. Stored in Atlas Vector Search. Consent recorded at this point. HR does nothing.

HR creates a new job requirement. Types the description in plain text. Gemma and sentence-transformers embed it. Atlas finds the top 10 semantic matches across the entire pool in under 2 seconds.

Before showing external results, the system checks current employees. Anyone suitable for promotion or lateral move is flagged at the top.

In parallel, APScheduler detects the new job was created. Finds top 50 candidates from 2 or 3 years ago who match. wacli sends each a personalized WhatsApp from your number. They reply with new CVs. FastAPI catches the reply. CV auto-parsed. Profile updated. Freshness score refreshed.

HR opens candidate profiles and chats with their CVs using Gemma.

If nobody fits internally, one click sends the JD to external portals. All applicants auto-imported back with consent. They join the permanent pool.

Every 30, 60, and 90 days the nudge engine runs automatically keeping the whole pool alive and fresh.

**Who benefits and how**

HR saves hours every day. No manual CV reading, no chasing candidates for updates, no reposting the same job every quarter. The system handles all of it.

The organization stops losing money on repeat Naukri subscriptions for roles they could have filled internally. Every past applicant becomes an asset instead of dead data.

Candidates get treated properly. Their consent is asked. They are notified when relevant roles open. They are not ignored because they applied two years ago.

The legal and compliance team has nothing to worry about. Every data point has a consent trail. DPDP 2025 compliant from day one.
